Key Messaging Points for Judge West

1The Records Are Fabricated
69% of all shifts are logged at exactly 8.00 hours. 98.5% of rest breaks are recorded at exactly 10 minutes. These aren't real-time records — they're synthetic entries created after the fact. OC admits 99% are handwritten. When the employer fabricates time records, Donohue shifts the burden to them to prove compliance.
2Zero Written Policies Across Both Entities
Defense counsel confirmed in writing (March 19): neither Magnolia Springs nor Mission Wells maintained any written meal, rest, or overtime policies during the entire class period. No policies = no affirmative defense under Brinker. There is nothing for them to point to showing they authorized and permitted compliant breaks.
3RROP Is an Independent, Clean Claim
DSPTrainingStipend and Bonus were excluded from the regular rate for computing overtime and sick pay. This isn't a factual dispute — it's visible on the face of every pay stub. Under Ferra v. Loews and Alvarado v. Dart Container, these non-discretionary payments must be included. Independent damages category that doesn't depend on violation rates.
4Rounding Systematically Underpaid
When the employer switched from synthetic to actual time punches in 2024, rounding shaved an average of 2.4 minutes per shift. The rounding was not neutral — it consistently favored the employer. California has moved away from rounding (Camp v. Home Depot), and these records prove the system wasn't neutral even by the old standard.
5$4.53M Is From Their Own Data
BCG's full exposure number ($4,532,504) is derived from defendant's own timekeeping and payroll records. This isn't hypothetical — it's computed from the data they produced. Every claim category (meal, rest, RROP, rounding, wage statements) is independently supported by their records.
6PAGA Adds Independent Penalty Exposure
120 aggrieved employees across 5,000 pay periods. Not arbitrable. PAGA penalties apply on top of the class damages for every violation category — meal, rest, RROP, rounding, and wage statements. Each is an independent penalty track. Settlement eliminates this entire exposure.
